Interview Experience

Day - 1

Round 1 & 2, June 25, 2019

Hii!! This is My First Interview Experience Blog..!!


I was really excited and happy that I started to write my first Blog and it was My First Interview Experience @Zilker Technology.


I had an idea to write a blog since from my 2nd year. But I couldn't make it off. I'll continue my personal in another blog and come lets start and thanks for joining with me here!!


That day on (25/06/2019) started as usual we were came late, so we didn't attend the ppt session. I was sitting near Krishna square and chitchat with my friends, later the session ended we moved to the labs and the drive started..


At First there were quant's apti(10), Technical apti(30), Programming Questions(2) for a total of 1hr 15min. I do completed all the questions and by solving 1 program successfully. We came out and discussed about how guys done!!. But many people said that didn't except Myself ad Narain. We gone to have breakfast.


We people moving to write face test and results came. We both got "Selected for the 2nd round". I was really happy that I'm gonna attend a Tech-Interview for the first time after got rejected from the prevoius companies in first round.


It took 4.30hrs to complete the 2nd round and I was afraid and a bit nervous that how I'm gonna speak there inside the interview. Later my name was called I gone into the room and It took 3-4[min] to make myself comfortable then the interviewer started with My Favourite Domains. I said more about Android and he asked to me to write a piece of code (a simple form process to get and display the text into the next page) I wrote the java and xml code and explained it clearly. Later he started asking me about my projects that i have mentioned in the resume. I said one by one and I showed my personal app installed in my mobile, he replied "COOL".


Later he started asking questions on java, data structures, problem solving and the domains which I was interested with. Then he moved to behavioural questions and puzzles and ended with a smart question of "Do U wanna ask anything to me?", I replied boldly the I didn't attend morning ppt session so can u please share me that and I asked about the company workculture, he replied clearly and I came out then moved to room and shared my experience with all people in the room and I found some mistakes that I have done there and I thought by changing the way I speak, and a small doubt that I won't move to next round and later the results came and "I was one amoung the 12".


Day - 2

Round 3, June 26, 2019

The next day (26/06/2019) I woke up early and started preparing for the tech-group discussion. I gathered more information from people who got into the company already and from my friends. Later I was inside the office for interview. They tested our Problem Solving skills by giving 3 questions that has to be solved within 1[hr] time. I completed 2 among from 3. We everyone waiting next to the panel room.


I was called as the first person. By getting inside there are not 2 or 3 people inside rather than 7. They started asking me questions on what I have solved in the paper & pen round, I explained clearly about my approach and the logic that I have used and later questions raiesed entirely from my Resume about family, domains specific, stress testing, behavioural questions and some personal talks about my knowledge about their company. I Learned Lots and Lots of things from that session that seems to be an [Ice-Breaking]. Some questions that made me to ask them { Oh God!! why can't you change the question comes in my mind!!}. And some questions made me to think the {Past, Present, Future} to put me in that situation to answer those related questions. Some questions made me to know who I'm. It took 40-50[min] to complete that session. Then the results came by evening 6 and I was not among the 7 selects.


I shared this experience with my friends so that I can come to know the mistakes that I have done, so that I'll change and upgrade myself for the upcomings..


Thanks for spending your time and happy to share!! Have a great day :)
